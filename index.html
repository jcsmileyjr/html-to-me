The Power of Meetups

Networking

It's not what you know but who you know
  • Meet, talk, and be seen by developers who work in the industry
  • As much as 80% of new jobs are never listed but are instead filled internally or via networking.
  • 70% - 85% of people ended up in their current position thanks to networking.

Community

You must want success as much as you want to breathe
  • Find a mentor
  • Listen to motivational talks
  • Get help with coding problems.
  • Collaborate in-person, in real-time, with people who have similar interests

Exposure

Build Learn Grow
  • Get inspire by projects and ideas
  • Learn about other programming languages like machine learning and R for data science
  • Opportunities like Hackathons and Volunteer jobs
  • Chance to talk to people doing the job you are interest in

Meetup Tips

  1. Put your phone on silent. Bring a notepad to take down key points.
  2. After the meetup, connect with the speakers, organizers, and other attendees. Bring business cards or ready to exchange contact information
  3. Make learning and the exchange of knowledge your primary goal
